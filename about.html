HISTORY

We’re presently

Conducting and 3 years Honours degree courses in Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA), Bachelor of Science in Biotechnology (B.Sc. Biotech) under the affiliation of The University of Burdwan with many more new courses likely to be added from the current academic session. Besides above short-term vocational courses on Computer Application and Financial Accounting (FA) Courses under the affiliation of West Bengal State Council of Technical Education (W.B.S.C.T. & VE & SD.) The Institute is designed in a congenial environment to develop one's own abilities under the constant guidance of a team of well-qualified, dedicated and experienced Faculties. The Management insists on a strict code of conduct for students and teaching staff fostering quality keeping in parity with the fundamentals of good education to contribute a better society of tomorrow.

Our Pride !

Cyber Research & Training Institute popularly known as CRTI (Affiliated to The University of Burdwan) emerged as a well knit organization with a vision to promote excellence in the field of Computer Science,Biotechnology and Management Studies in multisectoral and multidisciplinary perspectives.The framers of this Institute dreamed to implement the best of academic practices, so as to produce successful and enterprising professionals for taking up future challenges under globalized market economy.

It is a matter of great pride that the hard labour has paid and CRTI has become a dream-come-true establishment in reality to cherish.

Being proud of its large infrastructural facilities with a total area of 24000 sq.ft., the Institute stands centrally at Burdwan town.

The Railway Station within a five minute walking distance, the main market in close vicinity and the adjacent bus stand are added advantages and thus the Institute extends facilities to all in every possible ways.

Infrastructure

Design

Class Rooms

The classrooms are spacious, disturbance free, A.C., airy and separated by broad corridors. Sprawling learning zones for respective departments with labs and teachers' room nearby has been planned for convenience. The institute provides a well equipped conference room and an adjacent seminar hall with a corresponding corridor.

Laboratories and Experimental Facilities

CRTI boasts cutting-edge computer labs with the latest tech, internet access, and licensed software, supporting B.B.A., B.C.A., and Biotech students. Our IT lab features advanced gear like Microprocessor Trainer kits and Oscilloscopes, ensuring top-notch practical training. The biotech lab is equipped with high-end instruments, supporting B.Sc. Biotechnology studies, and our expert faculty ensures stellar academic performance.

Library

CRTI’s college library is a hub of knowledge, offering an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources across various disciplines. With a serene study environment, the library is equipped with modern amenities such as comfortable seating, high-speed internet, and computer terminals. Our library staff are always on hand to assist students in finding the right resources. Additionally, the library features dedicated sections for research, group study, and multimedia learning, ensuring a comprehensive support system for all academic needs.
